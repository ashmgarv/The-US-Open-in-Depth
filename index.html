The US Open in Depth

The Overview (first graph) shows us the knockout matches for every year (2004 - 2014), starting from the quarters to the final. Size of each circle represents the total points earned by the winning player in that particular match. Hovering on the bubbles gives name of the winner and round of the tournament.

On clicking on a bubble of the overview graph,the Detail (second graph) is generated which shows a Ladder Graph of the journey of the winning player in the US open for that particular year. Hovering on this rectangle box gives you name of the opposition in each match played and the match score

The third and the final visualization comes up on clicking on any rectangle box in the view and this shows us the different ways in which points were lost\gained in that particular match by the winner along with his total points. On hover, these bars give us the actual value of that Match performance variable. Below are the different point types explained.

Break %: break point conversion percentage
Return %: percentage of return points won
Total: total points won in the game
Winner: points won that were not received by the opponent
Error: the number of errors committed (hitting out of bounds, etc.)
Net %: when approaching the net, the percentage of points won
